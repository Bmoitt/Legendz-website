Our Story

For years, Tandeka and Wayne poured their love for cooking into every holiday, turning each gathering into a feast fit for royalty. Now they're bringing that same warmth and tradition to the streets — blending the rich traditions of Caribbean cuisine with the comfort of Southern cooking. Legendz is proudly Caribbean flavors with a Southern twist, served hot, right off the truck.
